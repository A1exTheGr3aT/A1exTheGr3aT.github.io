Welcome to the World of Exoplanets

Exoplanets, or extrasolar planets, are planets located outside our solar system. They orbit stars other than the Sun and come in various sizes, compositions, and distances from their stars. The study of exoplanets is crucial in understanding the formation and evolution of planetary systems and searching for potential life beyond Earth.

Explore the fascinating world of exoplanets and learn about their unique characteristics and the search for habitable worlds.
